Pulse Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 14, 2026

What We Collect

  • Location: Your device's GPS coordinates when you send a ping ("I'm Here") or have background location enabled. Coordinates are anonymized and aggregated into heatmap data — we do not store a personal movement history.
  • Account info: Username and email address (or phone number) used to create your account.
  • In-app activity: Venue pings, neighborhood predictions, and heat scores you accumulate.
  • Push token: A device token used to deliver opt-in notifications about trending venues.

What We Don't Collect

  • We do not sell your data to third parties.
  • We do not use your data for advertising targeting.
  • We do not build individual movement profiles.

How We Use Your Data

Location pings power the live heatmap. Account data lets you track your streak and predictions. Push tokens deliver notifications only when you have opted in.

Data Retention

Your data is stored in Supabase (US region). You may delete your account at any time from the Profile screen, which permanently removes all associated data.
